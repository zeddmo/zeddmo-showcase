# VenueOps Cloud

## Category
B2B SaaS for sports venue operators.

## Problem
Venue owners manage bookings, staffing, pricing, and utilization across fragmented tools, leading to revenue leakage and operational inefficiency.

## Solution Vision
A single operations cloud for scheduling, dynamic pricing, staff planning, and analytics.

## Core Modules
- Multi-tenant account and organization management
- Court/field inventory and schedule planning
- Dynamic pricing engine by time, demand, and season
- Staff shifts and operational checklist workflows
- Revenue, occupancy, and cancellation analytics dashboards

## Suggested Architecture
- Frontend: Next.js + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js services + PostgreSQL
- Jobs/automation: Redis queue workers
- Billing: Stripe subscriptions

## Showcase Deliverables
- Product architecture diagram
- Role-based UX map (owner, manager, staff)
- KPI model: occupancy, average booking value, repeat bookings
- Demo storyboard for investor/client presentation
