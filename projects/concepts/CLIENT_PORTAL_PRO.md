# Client Portal Pro

## Category
Agency and B2B collaboration product.

## Problem
Agencies and service teams lose efficiency when approvals, deliverables, invoices, and communication are spread across multiple systems.

## Solution Vision
A unified client portal with project tracking, approvals, deliverable management, and billing visibility.

## Core Modules
- Account and team permissions
- Project timeline and milestone tracking
- File handoff and approval workflow
- Invoice visibility and payment status
- Feedback history and decision logs

## Suggested Architecture
- Frontend: Next.js + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + PostgreSQL
- Cloud storage integration for deliverables
- CI/CD pipeline with environment separation

## Showcase Outcome
- Demonstrates client-facing product strategy
- Emphasizes collaboration and operational clarity
- Strong fit for consultancy portfolio positioning
