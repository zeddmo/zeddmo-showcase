# SaaS Operations Hub

## Category
B2B operations platform.

## Problem
Growing companies often manage billing, workflows, approvals, and reporting across disconnected tools, which increases errors and slows delivery.

## Solution Vision
A centralized operations hub for process management, approvals, SLA tracking, and executive reporting.

## Core Modules
- Workspace and role management
- Process templates and approval pipelines
- SLA dashboard and incident tracking
- Billing and subscription controls
- Executive analytics and audit trails

## Suggested Architecture
- Frontend: Next.js + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js services + PostgreSQL
- Background workers for automation and notifications
- Deployment: Docker + CI/CD pipelines

## Showcase Outcome
- Enterprise-style dashboard concept
- Strong case for workflow automation expertise
- Clear architecture and product ownership narrative
