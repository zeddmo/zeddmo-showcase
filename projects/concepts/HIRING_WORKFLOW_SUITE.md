# Hiring Workflow Suite

## Category
Recruitment operations platform.

## Problem
Hiring teams struggle with fragmented candidate data, inconsistent evaluations, and long decision cycles.

## Solution Vision
An end-to-end hiring workflow suite with pipeline automation, scorecards, and recruitment analytics.

## Core Modules
- Candidate pipeline management
- Interview planning and scorecards
- Hiring panel collaboration workspace
- Offer workflow and approval controls
- Analytics for time-to-hire and conversion quality

## Suggested Architecture
- Frontend: Next.js app router + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js API + PostgreSQL
- Background processing for notifications and reminders
- Reporting service for recruiting KPIs

## Showcase Outcome
- Demonstrates business workflow product design
- Highlights measurable operations impact
- Useful for HR tech and SaaS portfolio narratives
